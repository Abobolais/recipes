INGREDIENTS
160 g unsalted butter, softened (1.4 stick butter; 7/10 cup; 5.65oz)
160 g all-purpose flour (1 1/4 cup; 5.65oz)
160 g caster sugar (7/10 cup; 5.65oz)
3 eggs + 2 egg yolks
Pinch salt
2 teaspoons baking powder
50 g almonds (1/3 cup) (optional)**
* Get out of refrigerator 30 min before using

METHOD
Preparation Preheat the over at 190 degrees C. (th.6) and butter and flour the pan.

Prepare the cake batter

In a mixing bowl, whisk in flour and baking powder; set aside.

In a medium bowl, mix butter and sugar with a spatula for one minute. 
Add in eggs, egg yolks, and a pinch of salt. And whisk until well blended. 
This process is called emulsion. Fold in the flour mixture. Finally stir in almond slices.

Bake Pour into a pound cake pan. 
Bake for 45 minutes, or until golden brown and an insulted wooden stick comes out clean. 
Out of oven, let it cool in the pan.
